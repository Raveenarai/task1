Specifications

About

Samsung has been launched its first flip smartphones on 20th February 2020 called Samsung Galaxy Z Flip (Model number: SM-F700FZPDDBT). This is the first flip smartphone in India. The Galaxy Z Flip is a beautiful piece of hardware inside and out. It’s an engineering feat that Samsung has succeeded in cramming so much hardware into this phone.
The camera is really beautiful with comparable results to Samsung’s 2019 flagships. There’s no dedicated telephoto camera, but it’s one of the trade-offs that come with the limited space. Battery size is another one of these trade-offs, but even the lackluster battery life is something we can live with
The biggest concern is that folding smartphones are way too fragile — the screen scratches easily, you never know when something is going to find its way inside that hinge and ruin it, and there is no water resistance at all. These are a lot of practical downsides to something that costs so much money.

Display

Samsung Galaxy Z has a 6.70-inch touchscreen primary display with a resolution of 1080x2636 pixels at a pixel density of 425 pixels per inch (ppi). It has one of the features that a 1.10-inch as its second display, with a resolution of 112x300 pixels at a pixel density of 303 pixels per inch (ppi).

Camera

The Samsung Galaxy Z Flip has 12-megapixel primary camera with an f/1.8 aperture and a pixel size of 1.4-micron and another 12-megapixel camera with an f/2.2 aperture and a pixel size of 1.12-micron. The rear camera has features like autofocus. It has a 10-megapixel camera on the front for selfies, with an f/2.4 aperture and a pixel size of 1.22-micron.

Connectivity & sensors

About connectivity of the Samsung Galaxy Z Flip include Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac, GPS, Bluetooth v5.00, and USB Type-C. The phone comes with the following sensors like accelerometer, ambient light sensor, barometer, compass/ magnetometer, gyroscope, proximity sensor, and fingerprint sensor. The Samsung Galaxy Z Flip has face unlock features.

Price

The price of the Samsung Galaxy Z Flip is started at Rs. 109,999.
